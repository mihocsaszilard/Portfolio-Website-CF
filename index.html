About my educational history

Due to my feelings about the elementary math I decided to continue my high school studies in a field closely related with math and informatics. Of course this was a choise I regter and not becasue of the informatics.

After this less pleasant experience I departed in the direction of chemistry and started to learn the knack of the pharmacology in the University. Some months before graduation I got my first job at a multinational pharmaceutical company as a chemical operator at the Technological Development Department. I learned a lot of useful things and gained skills like precision, time management and patience but after two years I realized that I`m get stuck so it`s time to change.

So in September 2018 I switched gears and 2 weeks later moved in Germany and get a new job as a "Dachdecker". Some times later I moved in Bavaria and get another job, this time as an electrician. During this time I learned and made new connections, become aware of new possibilities.

One of this possibilities is the Web Developement, the coding, my childhood dream. Ever since I can remember I always was curious about the technology and now I started my journey on this path. Now I`m really excited and can`t wait to find my way and my role in the tech community.
